GASHING WOUND

Cooldown: 32s (24s if used while ENRAGED)

Slasher slices forward and unleashes a relentless chain of attacks with his machete and chainsaw. The effects are as follows:

  • Begins with two machete strikes, each dealing about 20% of the total damage.
  • Follows with a chainsaw stab, dealing about 10% of the total damage.
  • Finishes by kicking the survivor to the ground, causing a brief ragdoll effect.
  • Default total damage is 50, but if ENRAGED, damage is increased to 70.
  • Can strike multiple survivors within the hitbox.
  • Suffers from long endlag if the attack fails.

RAGING PACE

Cooldown: 30s

Upon activation, Slasher enters his ENRAGED state. The effects are as follows:

  • Speed becomes 19 for 14 seconds.
  • The aura of the nearest survivor is revealed until ENRAGED ends, lingering for an additional 2s afterward.
  • Slasher cannot get Stunned.
  • Sprinting is disabled.
  • Stamina gradually caps at 70 over 6.5s. (Cap is removed once ENRAGED ends.)
  • If Slasher uses Slash or Behead while ENRAGED, they lunge 19 studs forward in the direction they are facing.
